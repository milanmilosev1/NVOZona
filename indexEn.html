ZONA

About us

  Zona is a non-governmental non-profit organization founded in 2024 as an initiative of a former participant of the UWC course "Building a Sustainable Future", Miona Kosijer, in order to realize her post-course project. All members and participants who contribute to the work of the organization, from designing posts on social networks to the implementation of the events themselves, participate on a volunteer basis. Through its work, the organization will deal with topics such as preservation of the environment, activism, active citizenship, social and economic sustainability.

"SUSTAINABLE PATHS" PROJECT

 The project "Sustainable paths" is the first project that our organization is implementing. It was created as a post-course project of the founder of the organization, former participant of the UWC "Building a SustainableFuture" course, held at the Robert Bosch College located in Freiburg, Germany in the year 2023. The National Committee of UWC Deutschland supported and partly financed this project. The project consists of 5 workshops that will deal with the following topics:


  1. Introduction to sustainability⯈

    2. 23.5.2024. 20h

    covers sustainability-related vocabulary, concepts of sustainability, leading organizations and initiatives, global problems, elementary mechanisms, etc.

  2. Climate change⯈

    3. 31.05.2024 20h

    covers global warming, ecological footprints, weather and climate, greenhouse effect, gas emissions, consequences, etc.

  3. Food sustainability⯈

    4. covers food production, agricultural industry, ecological and social impacts of modern food production, recycling food waste, food recycling loop system, etc.

  4. Fashion sustainability⯈

    5. covers fast fashion, sweatshops, neocolonialism, consumerism, capitalism, etc.

  5. Activism ⯈

    6. covers spaces, faces and levels of power, what is activism, who is an activist, protests, successful actions, points of activist interventions, etc.


  The introductory part of the workshops will last between 20 and 30 minutes. During this time, the guest lecturer introduces the participants to the topic of the workshop. The rest of the time is devoted to activities through which the topic is further discussed and researched.


  Workshops are led and facilitated by high school and university students who have been engaged in this field for a long time through work and volunteerism. The workshops themselves are divided into an introductory part, which consists of a lecture during which participants have the opportunity to familiarize themselves with basic concepts and vocabulary related to the topic of the workshop, and to the interactive part where the topic is further discussed and explored through various activities. Throughout these workshops, attendees will be encouraged to practice their public speaking, debate and dialogue approaches and with it better their overall conversation skills that would prepare them for active participation and further discussions. The implementation of this project is planned to achieve a better understanding of the topic of sustainability amongst the youth in our local community and motivate them to continue learning and participating in this and topics such as this one. By doing this, we hope to create a spread of information amongst all citizens and make a long lasting change.
